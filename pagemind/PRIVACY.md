# Privacy Policy — PageMind AI

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## Overview

PageMind AI is a browser extension that summarizes webpages and enables AI-powered chat with page content. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

PageMind AI does **not**:
- Collect personal information
- Track your browsing history
- Store your API key on any server
- Send data to any server other than the AI API you configure
- Use cookies or analytics

---

## Data Handling

### API Key
Your Groq API key is stored **locally** in Chrome/Edge's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`) on your device only. It is never transmitted to any server other than Groq's API directly from your browser.

### Page Content
When you use PageMind AI to summarize or chat about a page, the text content of that page is sent directly from your browser to the Groq API. This data is governed by [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/).

### Chat History
Your chat history is stored **locally** on your device only and is never transmitted to any external server.

---

## Third Party Services

PageMind AI uses the **Groq API** to process page content. Please review Groq's privacy policy at [groq.com/privacy-policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/) to understand how they handle data.

---

## Your Control

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:
1. Opening PageMind AI
2. Clicking the ⚙ Settings icon
3. Clicking **Reset Extension**
